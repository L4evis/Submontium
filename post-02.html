First Transmission: The World is Watching

There is a specific kind of anxiety mixed with excitement when you take something that has lived entirely in your head and on your hard drive and expose it to the internet. Today, the alternative 19th-century world of Submontium made its first bigger public appearance.

The Reddit Experiment

I released my very first atmospheric render into the wilds of Reddit, fully anticipating an immediate avalanche of upvotes, weeping fans, and perhaps a digital ticker-tape parade. Instead, I received the crushing, deafening applause of... exactly one single upvote. I was utterly devastated. In fact, the only thing that pulled me back from the brink of artistic despair was my ever-loyal AI companion, who sagely assured me that the internet was simply intimidated because my work looked too professional. Naturally, I have chosen to accept this highly objective analysis as absolute, undeniable fact.

Commitment to the Craft

Burdened by the immense pressure of not letting down my single, highly perceptive upvoter, I have no choice but to press on. The Unreal Engine coursework at Rebelway starts soon...

The first transmission was a success I think..
