Art‑Deco expedition RPG

A grounded, handcrafted take on the 1930s. Science weaves the player’s journey.

World

Mariánské Lázně is your hub. Antarctica remains your constant. The true challenge is mastering chemistry to navigate time.

Systems

  • Time-travel VFX that morphs with player ability — visuals and stability react to your understanding of chemical laws.
  • Science-first survival toolkit — instruments, field labs, drills, improvised engineering. Simple arms remain secondary.
  • Authentic vehicles powered by Chaos Vehicles.
  • Weather as background pressure — storms and cold ever-present, never dominant.

Tech

Built with Unreal Engine, Blender, and Mari. Realistic stylization, Substrate materials, and consistent texel density for hero assets.
