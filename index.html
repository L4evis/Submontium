Design Pillars

  • Science over confrontation — no combat; progress through knowledge, tools, reactions, and experimentation.
  • Playable alchemy — distillation, calcination, dissolution, crystallisation; real procedures, systemic outcomes.
  • A real city as your board — patrons, guilds, scarcity, and political pressure in 17th-century Karlovy Vary.

Scope

  • First-person systemic RPG (no combat)
  • Engine: Unreal Engine 5.6
  • Platform: PC (Steam)
  • Status: In development
