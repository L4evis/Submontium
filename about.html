Art‑Deco expedition RPG

A grounded, handcrafted take on the 1930s. Memory, weather and science weave the player’s journey.

World

Start at the Mariánské Lázně colonnade hub. Travel by rail to Hamburg, ship to Buenos Aires, and onward to Patagonia. Choose a daring airship route or a classic sea passage to Antarctica.

Systems

  • Weather forecasting and planning.
  • Terrain deformation with Chaos Flesh on snow and ice.
  • Vehicles with Chaos Vehicles.
  • Non‑lethal toolkit: instruments, labs, drills, survival engineering.

Tech

Built with Unreal Engine 5.6.1, Blender 4.4+, and Mari 7.1+. Realistic stylization and consistent texel density for hero assets.
